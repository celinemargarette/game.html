BlockBlast

BlockBlast is a fun simple game that makes you arrange blocks in order!

5 reasons why this game is my favorite

  • It's free.
  • There are not many ads.
  • It's easy and fun to play.
  • You can play it to seem busy and avoid awkward situations.
  • You can play it offline.
